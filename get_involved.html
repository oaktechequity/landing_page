We're putting together a TechEquity Blueprint and a Charter to encourage Oakland Tech Employers to embed the following principles into their company culture:

Hire Locally
Offer Internships
Invest in Diversity
Offer volunteer and mentorship opportunities

Who are we?

We're a collective of folks from technology companies, technology education organizations and local community organizations. We live and/or work in Oakland, we love Oakland, and we want Oakland to stay Oakland.

What brings us together is the mission of building an inclusive, representative and community-oriented tech ecosystem in Oakland. We advocate for sustainable, equitable growth in Oakland and for policies that prevent displacement.
